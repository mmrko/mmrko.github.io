Hi, welcome to my site! I'm Marko — a web developer from Espoo, Finland with an organizer's mindset and a passion for all things web. I follow the web community actively trying to keep up with the bleeding edge. I'm 50% perfectionist, 50% pragmatic and 100% proactive person :) My secret ability is being able to shuffle cards with one hand (yes, I've had too much free time).

As I'm soon finishing my master's degree on Media Technology at Aalto SCI I'm eagerly looking forward to new opportunities. Lately I've gotten more involved in OSS development. I hope to keep contributing back to the community whenever I find the time (still searching).

Examples of my proudest & less proud work can be found under My Work. For my favorite developer mantra I'll quote Addy Osmani: “First do it, then do it right, then do it better”.

Technical lead in a site renewal project

Keywords: Drupal, Sass, shell scripting, RWD
During 2013-2014 I worked alongside my studies as the technical lead for a site renewal project of several smaller brands of A-lehdet, Finland's 3rd largest magazine company. One of the six sites I developed was Kotivinkki.fi, which averages around 30 000 weekly visitors.

First stab at a touch-friendly HTML5 game

Keywords: CSS animations, timeline/render profiling, CreateJS
As a course project at the university I developed an HTML5-based version of the legendary Nintendo game Duck Hunt. Much thought went into optimizing rendering performance as the goal was to make the game playable on touch-enabled devices. Click on the image to give the game a go!

Creating a mobile-friendly digital brochure experience

Keywords: jQuery Mobile, project coordination & collaboration, Radical Design Week
During my time at ThingLink I developed a mobile-friendly digital brochure for the Radical Design Week 2012 in Shanghai. I worked on the project in collaboration with WTF Helsinki, a creative digital agency from Helsinki, carrying the responsibility for the technical implementation and project coordination.

Aalto Arts New Media & Production major entrance exam submissions

Keywords: D3.js, Canvas API, Twitter API, Natural Earth, infovis
In 2013 and 2014 I applied for the New Media & Production major at Aalto Arts. On both occasions I fell just a few places short but earned a reserve spot for the program by finishing in the top 20 (out of ~110 applicants).

In the 2013's exam the goal of the creative task was to visualize information flood in the contemporary society using Auguste Rodin's Thinker as a source of inspiration. Respectively, in 2014 the task was to depict the concept of Digital Divide using any given medium. I chose the browser of course :)

Volunteering as a best buddy for the mentally challenged

Keywords: Making the world a better place
In fall 2013 I began volunteering as a best buddy for a mentally challenged young man. We get together once or twice a month to hang out, cook food or to watch a movie. Working as a research assistant at the university

Keywords: Matlab, qualitative analysis, research experiment setup
I worked as a research assistant in the Visual Media Research Group at Aalto SCI during 2010-2012. The research topics revolved around evaluating the effects of different stereoscopic stimuli and measuring the readability of concave/convex displays. One the research projects focused on the effect of image scaling on stereoscopic movie experience.
